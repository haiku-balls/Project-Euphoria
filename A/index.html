How exactly do rings work? What are their benefits?

The Internal Ring is for me to make new things, and break everything.

  • You're lucky the website isn't unusable. That's about it for you.
    •

The External Ring is basically canary for you.

  • You get to try the newest things.
  • There will be issues.
  • Experiments are enabled.
    •

The banana ring is for Kris.

Finally, the Stable Ring is for everyone, but this time stable.

  • You get to try the newest things, when they're ready.
  • There will be little to no issues.
  • Experiments are disabled.
    •

The hierarchy is Internal < External < Stable

How do these translate to branches?

The Internal Ring doesn't have a branch.

The External Ring uses the 'dev' branch.

The Stable Ring uses the 'master' branch.
